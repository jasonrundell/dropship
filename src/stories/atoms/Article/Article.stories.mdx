# Article

## The HTML `<article>` element.

The Article component is used to create a article element and group content.

<ArgsTable story="Default" />

export const Template = (args) => <Article {...args} />

export const Example = () =>
  'The stadium was alive with excitement as the crowd cheered and chanted in unison. The teams took to the field, each determined to come out on top. The air was thick with the scent of popcorn and hot dogs, and the sound of vendors shouting out their wares added to the lively atmosphere. As the game progressed, tensions rose and fell with each play. When the final whistle blew, the stadium erupted in a thunderous applause. It was a moment of pure exhilaration, and for the fans, it was a memory that would last a lifetime.'
